about me

My name is Elizabeth Hartley and I am a full stack developer based in Wiltshire. I completed the full stack course at iO Academy in May 2024. Throughout this course I have developed a keen interest in front-end development and enjoy making websites look aesthetically pleasing and easy to use for the user. I have had many years of previous experience in the veterinary world as a Registered Veterinary Nurse and felt it was time for a change and a new challenge.
